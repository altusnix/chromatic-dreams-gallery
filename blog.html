The Evolution of Glass: From Fire to Form

December 15, 2024 by Dreams Meow 8 min read

Working with glass has taught me that transformation requires both heat and patience. Each piece begins as raw material—silica, soda ash, limestone—ingredients that seem ordinary until they meet the forge's embrace.

In my latest series, I've been exploring the relationship between control and surrender. The molten glass has its own will, its own flow. My role isn't to dominate but to guide, to suggest rather than demand. This dance between artist and medium mirrors the deeper transformations we undergo in life.

"Glass teaches us that beauty often emerges from the space between structure and chaos, between what we plan and what the material decides to become."
